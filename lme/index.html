Programs & Schedules

Find out More

Program 1

Little Mandarin

From under 3 years to 5 years old

Little Mandarin is designed for little kids to explore Chinese culture and Chinese language skills through stories, crafts, songs, role play and lots of interactive activities.

Program 2

First Step Mandarin

From under 5 years to 13 years old

First Step Mandarin is especially for students who have never had the experience of learning Mandarin and want to get started to learn the fundamentals of Mandarin. This course is suitable for students who want to learn Pinyin and basic Chinese characters.

Program 3

Fun Pinyin

5 + years old

Fun Pinyin is specially created for students who have had little or no experience with Pinyin previously.

Program 4

Mandarin Success

From under 6 years to 12 years old

Mandarin Immersion is designed for students who can speak Mandarin fluently but want to become proficient in reading and writing. Students should at least have the ability to read pinyin. A placement test will be conducted before the student joins the class. Students will be placed in a class based on age and placement test result. Our instructors will speak Mandarin only in class, and also we require students to speak Mandarin always during instruction time.

Program 5

Custom Your Learning

Any age

Whether you have a Chinese background or not, or a working professional aiming to attain basic language skills, our Custom Your Learning is tailored to reach your academic, professional and personal goals.
