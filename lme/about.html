Our Creative Minds

Lisa Luan

Senior Instructor

Lisa Luan is a native speaker of Chinese who was born and raised in China. She holds a Master's degree in Teaching Chinese to Speakers of Other Languages, and she has been teaching Chinese as a second language for 12 years. Ms. Luan had taught Chinese at Middlebury College Summer School and Allegheny College in the U.S.A. during the past few years. After completing her Master of Education degree from Lakehead University in 2018, Ms. Luan settled in Oakville with her family and decided to continue to pursue her passion for teaching Chinese to young learners. As a dedicated educator, her classes are always encouraging, interactive, and intensive. Ms. Luan believes that a good instructor does not tell students answers, but knows how to provide students with opportunities to find answers by themselves.
