Programs & Schedules

同步中国小学语文课程

From under 6 years to 12 years old

Elementary Students: this course is designed for the kids who can listen to Chinese well but can not read and write.

Find out More

拼音入门课程

From 6 years to 12 years old

This course is designed for the kids who have never learnt Pinyin. 30 hours for this course, and every class is 50 minutes.

Find out More

汉字入门课程

From 6 years to 12 years old

This course is highly recommended for the kids who have never learnt basic Chinese characters or who can not write Chinese characters in a proper order.

Find out More

诗歌班(Classic Poems)

From 6 years to 18 years old

This course is highly recommended for the kids who love to read Chinese poems and who is passionate about challenging herself/himself to memorized poems for fun.

Find out More

中国文化班(Chinese Culture)

From 6 years to 18 years old

Kids will learnt Chinese culture through different themes in each class. The themes include traditional Chinese holidays, Chinese cuisine, famous Chinese people, cities in China and so forth.

Find out More

成人初级班(Modern Standard Chinese)

from 18 years above

This course is highly recommended for the learners who is above 18 years old and students will learn chinese by using the textbooks, called Integrated Chinese which is used in most of the universities in the United States and Canada.
